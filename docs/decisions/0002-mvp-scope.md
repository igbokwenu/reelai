# ADR 0002: MVP Scope

Date: July 5, 2026

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Reel AI can become a large video production platform, but the hackathon submission needs a reliable, demonstrable system. Prior submissions were criticized for being local-only, thin wrappers, cloned examples, or over-scoped demos that did not clearly show agent behavior.

## Decision

The MVP ships:

- Brand Kit generation.
- Three parallel creative concepts.
- Concept selection.
- Editable storyboard.
- Keyframes for 2 to 4 scenes.
- Image-to-video generation for 2 to 4 scenes.
- Qwen TTS narration.
- 9:16 Remotion final render.
- Safe-zone captions and optional AI disclosure.
- Alibaba Cloud deployment proof.

The MVP excludes:

- Lip sync.
- Full generated music.
- Multi-format export.
- Spokesperson mode.
- Full ad-platform publishing.
- Multi-user billing.
- Guaranteed 60-second render.

## Consequences

Benefits:

- The demo is reliable and fits 1 to 3 minutes.
- The product still feels original because of concept pitching, Brand Kit reuse, continuity control, and visible orchestration.
- The implementation remains attainable for AI agents and human review.

Tradeoffs:

- Some impressive features become stretch work.
- The first public version is optimized for short reliable reels, not every possible content workflow.
